## 🖥️ Pre-Recording Checklist

### Environment
- [ ] Clean desktop — no unrelated files or icons
- [ ] Close all unnecessary tabs and applications
- [ ] Disable notifications (macOS: Do Not Disturb)
- [ ] Good lighting on face (if using webcam)
- [ ] Quiet room — no background noise
- [ ] Test microphone — clear audio, no echo

### Application
- [ ] Backend running: `cd backend && mvn spring-boot:run`
- [ ] Frontend running: `cd frontend && npm run dev`
- [ ] Open Chrome to http://localhost:5173
- [ ] Clear browser cache (Cmd+Shift+R)
- [ ] Verify no console errors (Cmd+Option+J)
- [ ] Ensure app is responsive and fast
- [ ] Pre-register a demo user or use Demo Mode

### Browser Settings
- [ ] Chrome / Edge — clean profile (no extensions)
- [ ] Bookmarks bar hidden (Cmd+Shift+B)
- [ ] Window maximized (1920×1080)
- [ ] DevTools closed
- [ ] Zoom at 100%

## 📋 Scene-by-Scene Recording Guide

### Scene 1: Opening Hook (0:00–0:15)

**Visual:** Full app showing sidebar navigation

**Narration:** *"60% of engineering graduates struggle with employability — because their preparation tools are scattered across a dozen platforms with no unified way to measure readiness. CareerCompass AI fixes that with one AI-powered platform."*

**Cursor Actions:**
- None — let the app load naturally
- Subtle mouse movement to show sidebar

**Timing Cue:** 15 seconds

---

### Scene 2: Demo Mode (0:15–0:25)

**Visual:** Topbar buttons → Demo Mode click

**Narration:** *"One click pre-fills everything — name, degree, target role, skills, resume text, and interview answer — all with realistic demo data. Perfect for judges who want to see the full flow instantly."*

**Cursor Actions:**
- Move cursor to **🎯 Demo Mode** button
- Click deliberately — show ripple effect
- Watch fields populate

**Timing Cue:** 10 seconds

---

### Scene 3: Generate AI Plan (0:25–0:45)

**Visual:** Click Generate → Loading state → Results animate in

**Narration:** *"Click 'Generate AI Plan.' Our backend calls the Gemini API, analyzes the full profile, and returns a structured career plan in seconds — readiness score, top career matches, skill gaps with priorities, and a personalized week-by-week roadmap."*

**Cursor Actions:**
- Hover on **Generate AI Plan** button
- Click → show loading state ("Generating...")
- Wait for results to appear
- Slowly move cursor down through results

**Timing Cue:** 20 seconds

---

### Scene 4: Readiness Score & Career Matches (0:45–1:05)

**Visual:** Score ring + career matches list

**Narration:** *"The Live Readiness Score animates to show the aggregate — 64 out of 100. Below, the top career match is highlighted: Java Full Stack Developer at 87% fit, with a reasoned explanation. Four additional career paths show fit percentages."*

**Cursor Actions:**
- Circle the score ring
- Point to each career match
- Hover over meter bars

**Timing Cue:** 20 seconds

---

### Scene 5: Skill-Gap Matrix (1:05–1:20)

**Visual:** Scroll to skill-gap panel

**Narration:** *"Scrolling down, the Skill-Gap Matrix shows a color-coded heatmap across nine core skills. Red means critical — DSA, Cloud, and Communication at 46%. Green means ready — like Java and Spring Boot if they're in your skillset. Each gap has an actionable recommendation."*

**Cursor Actions:**
- Scroll down smoothly
- Point to each priority level (Critical → Improve → Ready)
- Hover on heat-track bars

**Timing Cue:** 15 seconds

---

### Scene 6: AI Roadmap (1:20–1:35)

**Visual:** Roadmap panel with week-by-week list

**Narration:** *"The AI Roadmap gives you a structured 6-week plan: Week 1 sets your baseline and target companies. Week 2 practices your weakest skills. Week 3 builds project proof. Week 4 optimizes your resume. By Week 6, you're in placement sprint mode."*

**Cursor Actions:**
- Scroll through roadmap items
- Point to week numbers
- Highlight actions list

**Timing Cue:** 15 seconds

---

### Scene 7: Resume Intelligence (1:35–2:00)

**Visual:** Resume panel → Click Analyze → Scores appear

**Narration:** *"Resume Intelligence scores your resume in three dimensions: ATS keyword match at 78, content quality at 82, and impact measurement at 74. The AI identifies strengths, suggests fixes — like 'add measurable outcomes' — and provides rewrite examples you can copy directly."*

**Cursor Actions:**
- Point to resume text already filled
- Click **Analyze Resume** button
- Point to three score cards
- Scroll through fix suggestions
- Point to rewrite examples

**Timing Cue:** 25 seconds

---

### Scene 8: Mock Interview AI (2:00–2:25)

**Visual:** Interview panel → Click Score → Feedback appears

**Narration:** *"Mock Interview AI presents a STAR-format question. The candidate's answer is automatically scored on structure, specificity, and relevance — 71 out of 100. The verdict is 'Strong answer' with three coaching tips and an improved STAR-formatted answer."*

**Cursor Actions:**
- Point to the question
- Point to the filled answer
- Click **Score Answer** button
- Point to score, verdict, coaching tips
- Highlight the improved answer

**Timing Cue:** 25 seconds

---

### Scene 9: Admin Dashboard (2:25–2:45)

**Visual:** Sidebar → Click Admin Dashboard → Stats load

**Narration:** *"The Admin Dashboard gives placement cells and colleges a cohort-level view: total users, career plans generated, resumes analyzed, interviews conducted, average readiness score, and recent signups. A user table shows individual registration dates."*

**Cursor Actions:**
- Move cursor to sidebar
- Click **Admin Dashboard**
- Wait for stats to load
- Circle each stat card
- Point to user table

**Timing Cue:** 20 seconds

---

### Scene 10: Closing (2:45–3:00)

**Visual:** Full app view → Fade to thank you

**Narration:** *"CareerCompass AI turns career readiness from guesswork into a quantified, AI-powered journey. Seven modules. One platform. Built in 48 hours with Java, Spring Boot, React, and Gemini AI. Thank you — I'm happy to answer any questions."*

**Cursor Actions:**
- None — hold steady
- Smooth zoom out or fade

**Timing Cue:** 15 seconds

## 🎵 Audio Guide

### Voiceover Tips
- **Pace:** Slow and deliberate — not rushed
- **Tone:** Confident, enthusiastic, professional
- **Volume:** -6dB average, peaks at -3dB
- **Pauses:** 1-second pause between scene transitions
- **Emphasis:** Bold key numbers ("64 out of 100", "87% fit")

### Background Music Suggestions
| Track Type | Example | Volume |
|------------|---------|--------|
| Ambient corporate | "Inspiring Technology" by Uppbeat | -20dB |
| Light electronic | "Growing Up" by Scott Buckley | -22dB |
| Minimal cinematic | "Dreams" by Bensound | -18dB |

### Sound Effects
- **Button click:** Single tick sound (light UI click)
- **Score animation:** Rising tone (optional)
- **Page scroll:** Whoosh (very subtle, optional)
